Ornstein-Uhlenbeck process

A random walk in (1D) overdamped environment (inertia is negligible, so "there's no motion without a force.")

The parameters γ and σ control the magnitudes of forces: -γx drives the particle towards equilibrium position at x = 0, and ση(t) is the random force with variance σ2.
